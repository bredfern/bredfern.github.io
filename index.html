About

I am an LA based web developer, I have worked with many large companies over the years as a web developer using various technologies, php, c#, python, nodejs.

Currently I am learning rust looking at a move from web development to embedded device rust development (bare metal).

I had an unusual journey into software. As a grad student at CalArts I was interested in live performance online.

My first language was Java, I taught myself in order to use it to generate digital art and enable me to live stream on the web. Today java is still at the core of many live stream tools such as the Red5 server and many others.

My first job out of CalArts was with Geocities on their pre-ipo team.

Projects

Most recently I have been upgrading my skills working heavily on learning Rust, hopefully to get good enough to start making some major controbutions to free software projects written in Rust.

In the past I wrote java for Disney and have a cool Imagineering pin from my time with them.

Before the pandemic I got into webrtc and worked on a number of webrtc programs.

I have worked with many databases over the years, MS SQL Server, Oracle, Mysql, Postgresql, Mongodb, and now I am digging into sqlite hoping to work on the project to rewrite sqlite in Rust.
